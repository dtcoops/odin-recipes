Beef and Broccoli

image courtesy of: https://www.flickr.com/photos/joyosity/28265304420

Description

Who needs take out when you can make this? Slices of beef and broccoli served in a rich sauce on top of rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • cornstarch
  • flank steak
  • low sodium soy sauce
  • minced garlic
  • fresh ginger
  • vegetable oil
  • brocolli
  • white onion

Directions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Make a sauce
  3. Cook beef then set aside
  4. Cook onion and broccoli in the same pan
  5. Return beef and sauce to pan
  6. Plate and nom!
