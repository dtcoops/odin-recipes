Brussel Sprout Taco

Description

Taco Tuesday?! Try something a little different. Sauted brussel sprouts with black beans and corn.

Ingredients

  • taco shell
  • brussel sprouts
  • taco seasoning
  • corn
  • jalapeno
  • lime
  • cilantro
  • black beans
  • red onion
  • feta

Directions

  1. Saute brussel sprouts with taco seasoning
  2. Saute corn with jalapeno. Add cilantro and lime once corn is browned.
  3. Cook beans with juices until sauce thickens. Add Lime, cilantro, and japaleno
  4. Assemble and nom!
